Community websites

Plakar's logo has not yet been widely adopted or reused by third parties. Nevertheless, we wish to clarify our position early to avoid confusion in the future. These guidelines are not intended to prohibit or discourage legitimate community uses that show support for Plakar.

The purpose of this policy is mainly to prevent misuse of the Plakar name and logo in a way that could be unfair, misleading, or intended to take advantage of Plakar's reputation and community for commercial gain without authorization.

In particular, we want to ensure that the Plakar brand is not used to promote commercial services or products that may suggest an official partnership or endorsement, or that could create confusion among users about whether an offering is operated by Plakar or by independent third parties.

If you are unsure whether your use of the Plakar name or logo is appropriate, feel free to contact us. We are committed to supporting genuine community initiatives and will do our best to provide guidance.
