Why backing up Notion is your responsibility

Notion guarantees platform availability and security but does not provide full backups of your workspace content. Any accidental deletion, API glitch or malicious change becomes permanent without a user-managed backup.

As a Notion user, it is up to you to protect your own data. Failing to back up can result in irretrievable loss of critical pages, databases, media files and comments.

Your backup duties

  • Schedule automatic backups of all pages, databases, media and comments
  • Select and configure your preferred storage destination
  • Set retention periods and rotate old backups regularly
  • Secure your backups with strong credentials and encryption
  • Restore content to the same or another workspace whenever needed
